Droids I'd hang with

  1. R2D2

    R2D2 is probably the most famous of droids. He has appeared in every movie and series. The stories he could tell. Being an astromech, he'd be good around the house. He'd be one good designated driver, too! He could help with some Python code, as well.

  2. BB8

    BB8 is the new kid on the block, but shows a lot of potential. He's like a cute, little baby droid. He is inquisitive and a quick learner. He is state of the art and one of a kind. And, he's pretty well rounded. He also knows a lot about Css and html.

  3. C1-10P

    C1-10P is also known as Chopper or Specter 3. He's an older model for an asrtromech, but still functions well. Although he's not very brave, he can be greatly resourceful. He is spunky and says whatever goes through his ciruits. Maybe, it's about Github.

Jedi I'd like to meet

  1. Obi-wan Kenobi

    Obi-wan, or later known as Ben, Kenobi was a great Jedi knight, master, teacher. You either loved him or hated him. That's funny because that is Sith mentality, and Sith Lords were his speciality. He could mind trick our way to good seats anywhere. These are the seats we're looking for.

  2. Yoda

    Yoda, the Grandmaster of the Jedi order, would be a good guy to know. He's 900 years old, so you'd always look young next to him. Plus, you can take him to the casino for some preminitions, or maybe the lottery.

  3. Luke Skywalker

    Luke Skywalker is the son of Anakin, and, maybe, the most powerful Jedi ever. He could teach me how to fly spaceships and how to blow up really, really big stuff. He could also keep my new droid buddies in working order.

Planets I'd visit

  1. Coruscant

    Coruscant, the galactic capital, is one big city over the whole planet, with many levels to explore. Sites to see include the Senate Building and the Jedi Temple. The lower levels have spicier adventures awaiting.

  2. Kashyyyk

    Kashyyyk is a forest world and the home of the Wookies. I imagine it would be like camping with hippies. Making friends with Wookies could provide some muscle if I have to move.

  3. Endor

    Endor, sometimes called the Forest Moon or the Sanctuary Moon, is home to the Ewoks. Who wouldn't want to hang out with Ewoks. Maybe, you take one home. And, I heard Endor is a good place to blow stuff up

    The Millennium Falcon is a Corellian YT-1300f light freighter. It's the fastest ship in the fleet, and I want to do the Kessel run in   <   12 parsecs. And this ship has scanner proof smuggling compartments. (Don't ask me my business)

  2. X-Wing Starfighter

    The T-65B X-wing starfighter would be awesome to fly.   "Red 5, standing by."  Wings open, wings closed. Wings open, wings, closed, etc. You could blow up asteroids and pretend that they are the Death Star.

    The Ghost is a modified VCX-100 light freighter. It has engines which are baffled, energy dampeners, and static jammers to make it hard to detect. Basically, you never have to worry tickets. And it has a second ship for getting around town. It's owner is a Twi'lek, Hera, who i would like to meet. ;)
